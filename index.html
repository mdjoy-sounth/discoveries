In a quiet village, a calm and good-hearted boy lived alone. But the people from his clan constantly watched him and kept disturbing him. During this time, his clan members discovered something called Ovjarb, and they began announcing his every move out loud.

He never asked them, “Why are you bothering me?”

He already knew their answer. They would lie and say, “You have a problem in your head.”

This harassment had a long history. His great-grandfather had never given clan property to anyone, and because of that, the clan members never missed a chance to annoy him, even since childhood.

Now, they started bothering him even more because he had begun studying in class 10. They wanted to pressure him so much that he would leave the village and go back to his family.

Their plan was simple:

After he left, they would pretend to care. They would ask him why he went away and try to spread rumors that he had mental problems. Later, they would say he suddenly left without telling anyone the reason.

He understood their intentions very well, so he stayed silent and ignored everything.

In school, many girls liked him because he was calm, kind, peaceful, and rarely showed strong emotions. Most of the pretty girls admired him. Even teachers and students found him interesting because his personality was different from everyone else.

Because of this, every Friend, the villagers became jealous of him. And after hearing about his life, the whole village became jealous and started bothering him even more. Even students from his school joined them, trying to figure out why he was different.

Their use of Ovjarb reached a new level. They started predicting his thoughts. Their voices reached him through speakers placed around the village, or through people using their mobile phones to send their voices to him.

They accidentally discovered another method called Sounth, and from then on, they kept disturbing him using both Ovjarb and Sounth together.

After Sounth spread through the village, even vehicle drivers started saying her life through the horns of their vehicles.

Wherever he went, he heard their voices from outside the vehicles, they following him everywhere.

After one full year of harassment, they accidentally discovered Visual Mind Illusion.  Then they started showing him obscene dreams using Visual Mind Illusion to weaken him.

Nearly thirty months passed.

And still, they did not stop bothering him.
